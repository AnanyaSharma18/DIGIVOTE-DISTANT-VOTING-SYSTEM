Upcoming General Elections 2025

Overview

The General Elections 2025 are just around the corner. This is your chance to shape the future of our country. Make sure you are registered and ready to vote!

Eligibility

To be eligible to vote in the General Elections 2025, you must:

  • Be a citizen of the country.
  • Be at least 18 years old on election day.
  • Have a valid voter ID or registration.

How to Register

  1. Visit the official voter registration website.
  2. Fill out the registration form with accurate details.
  3. Submit the form and wait for confirmation.

Important Dates

  • Voter Registration Deadline: March 15, 2025
  • Election Day: May 20, 2025

Why Vote?

Voting is a fundamental right and a vital part of democracy. By voting, you have a say in the decision-making process and help elect leaders who represent your values and interests.
